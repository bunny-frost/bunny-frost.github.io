About GI AR EXP Calculator:

This GI AR EXP Calculator helps Genshin Impact players estimate the amount of experience needed to reach their desired Adventure Rank. It takes into account the current Adventure Rank and experience points, as well as the target Adventure Rank.

Feel free to use this calculator to plan your progression in the game!
